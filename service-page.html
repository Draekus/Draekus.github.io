Our Services

Joining BHealth will give you access to a wide range of health services.

Community Support

We have community forums and events where people can get together and share their experiences, meal plans, and work-out routines.

Workout Planning & Tracking

Get help planning your work-out or share your own routines with the community and gain detailed insights into your progress over time with our analytics dashboard.

Competitions

Compete for the top spots on the monthly leaderboards for a chance to win prizes like BHCC Merch and even a FitBit.

Nutritionists & Personal Trainers

We offer access to personal trainers and nutritionists who can help guide you on your journey to a healthier lifestyle.

Meal Planning

Eating healthy can be difficult but it doesn't have to be. Get daily customized meal plans to help you stay on track whether it be to gain muscle or lose weight.

Counseling

Mental and physical health are deeply connected so we provide access to counseling if you ever feel like you need someone to talk to.
