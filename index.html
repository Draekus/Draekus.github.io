BHCC BHealth

A community focused program promoting healthy relationships and lifestyles on the Bunker Hill campus.

What We're About

We connect BHCC community members with the services and support they need to live happier healthier lives.

Be Healthy with BHealth

BHealth provides a wide range of services from in-depth analytics on your diet and gym activity to personal trainers and counselors. We are a holistic program that aims to promote both physical and mental health through community support. 

Features

Check out these sweet membership perks!

Instant Access

Only a student ID number is required to sign-in to our app and gain access to the fitness center. No need for a student ID which means one less barrier between you and your fitness goals.

Analytics

Track your health journey. BHealth provides an analytics dashboard where you can track your progress, set goals, and see the top performers in the community.

Nutrition & Meal Planning

Connect with nutritionists and food-insecurity services to ensure you're eating healthy. Share your best recipes and meal plans with other BHCC community members.

Community Support

Getting healthy is hard, so let's do it together! BHealth provides a platform where you can share workout plans, ask for advice, or even just share your story.
